Exposure Therapy

Our Product
Connectome first product tackle a variety of different problems connected to obesity and binge eating. Connectome is a personalized app developed by healthcare professionals that uses virtual reality to promote weight-loss. Individuals are placed in real life scenarios to expose them to the eating decisions they face on a day-to-day basis.

Connectome works alongside leading psychitrists and clinicians to create effective therpaies. Our product is based on more than 20 years of scientific research. Over 60 clinical studies have shown that virtual reality is more effective than cognitive behavioral therapy as it allows individuals to develop a healthier relationship with food.
VR improves on current CBT treatments allowing effective and sustainained lifestyle changes.
Functions Include:

Experience real-life scenarios from the comfort your own home

Progress tracking

Smart food balance-sheets

Improve understanding of nutritional values of meals

Meal-plans customized to a users weight-loss plan and goals
